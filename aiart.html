Ai Art

For this project I used RunwayML to combine images. The main goal of this project is that I wanted to merge famous classical painters' portraits with "classic" 3D animated movies. I feel as time goes by, movies are starting to become classics despite being made within the 21st century. Therefore I decided to merge the two as a way of expressing how these movies have aged like fine wine.
