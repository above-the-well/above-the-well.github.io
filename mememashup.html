Mashing Up Two Memes


For this project I used photoshop to mash up two memes to create a new one. I combined the "ah shit here we go again" meme with the floating boy chasing running boy meme. I just wanted to make a meme about how people thought the pandemic and other disasters would be over once 2021 started. However, these problems have yet to disapear and we still have to deal with them.
